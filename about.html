About

After years of breaking down complex ideas and creating engaging learning experiences, I’m now bringing that same energy into the world of software engineering. I love solving problems, building things that work (and make sense!), and thinking creatively under pressure. Teaching taught me how to connect with people — now I want to build tools that do the same!
